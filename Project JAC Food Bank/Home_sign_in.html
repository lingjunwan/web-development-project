The JAC Pantry provides food assistance to support John Abbott College students in need. Over the years JAC food pantry have helped needy students through community and student involvement. JAC food bank is now accessible online. The online service is free for the eligible students. This platform will allow more students to use the food bank services. Students can place their orders and they can be either picked up from campus or can be delivered to them through our volunteers. The information collected on this website is not shared with any third party. JAC food bank organize fund rising events. Please see news section for upcoming events. Contact us for more information.
